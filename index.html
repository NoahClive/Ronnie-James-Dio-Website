Born Ronald James Padavona, Dio was an American vocalist and songwriter known for his distinctive and powerful voice as well as an impressive and varied collection of songs across 50 years. Dio was the vocalist in many iconic bands such as Elf, Rainbow, Black Sabbath, Dio, and Heaven & Hell.


Ronnie James Dio's discography spans over five decades and includes albums from all of his major bands. From Elf, Rainbow, Black Sabbath, Dio, and Heaven & Hell, Dio's discography is a testament to his creativity and lasting influence.
